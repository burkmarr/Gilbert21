Record Propeties form

Gilbert 21 - record properties form

The Record Properties form is where the individual record properties (field values) may be entered and edited. The form is implemented as a 'tabbed dialog'. Each of the tabs is described separately:


  • Who tab
  • What tab
  • Where tab
  • When tab
  • Commentary tab
  • Media tab
